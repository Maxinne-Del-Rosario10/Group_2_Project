Alcohol consumption is often glamorized in mainstream media, portrayed as a gateway to fun, relaxation, and social acceptance. However, this depiction can be dangerously misleading, especially for impressionable youth. Our advocacy focuses on raising awareness about the negative impacts of alcohol use, particularly among young people. We aim to highlight the health risks, potential for addiction, and the long-term consequences that alcohol can have on lives. By challenging the glorified image of alcohol in media, we strive to promote healthier, more informed choices for future generations. Explore our resources to learn more about the reality of alcohol consumption and how we can make a difference together.


We are a dedicated group of individuals committed to reducing the portrayal of alcohol as a positive influence in mainstream media. Our mission is to protect the youth from the harmful effects of alcohol by advocating for more responsible media representations. We believe that by educating the public and promoting honest conversations about alcohol, we can help prevent addiction and promote healthier lifestyles. Join us in our efforts to create a society where young people can make informed decisions free from the undue influence of glamorized alcohol use. Together, we can make a change.
